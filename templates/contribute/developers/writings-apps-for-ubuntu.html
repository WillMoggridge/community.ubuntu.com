Writing apps for Ubuntu

If you are interested in developing apps that run on the Ubuntu platform rather than contributing to the platform itself, head out to the App Developer Site . There you will find all the information and resources to get started writing your first app in a matter of minutes:

  • Learn about writing Ubuntu apps that run across devices and form factors
  • Ask your development questions on Ask Ubuntu

Follow the @ubuntuappdev social media account to stay up to date on events, initiatives and general news. Online you can find the blog on the App Developer Site , our Google+ community , or our Facebook , Twitter or Google+ pages .
