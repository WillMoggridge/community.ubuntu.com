We want Ubuntu to be great across all kinds of devices — a great phone and tablet experience. It is an open source platform which will power a wide range of devices. In no time it has attracted an enthusiastic and vibrant community, which works both on the platform itself and porting it to new devices.

If you have any questions, you can easily get them answered either by

  • joining us on IRC in #ubuntu-touch on irc.freenode.net and/or
  • joining our mailing list by joining the ubuntu-phone team on Launchpad and leaving the “Subscribe me to the team’s mailing list” option checked.

Porting

We want to port Ubuntu to all kinds of devices. If you have experience in porting code to Android devices or are generally knowledgeable in terms of porting, working with the Kernel and other core parts of a distribution, this might be interesting to you.

Ubuntu platform

You can very easily help keep Ubuntu’s platform moving by testing it and reporting bugs or by getting involved in writing and fixing its apps and infrastructure bits.
